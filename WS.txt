A sequel, improvement and rewrite of landocalrissian's original [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3704210589]Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 2009 HUD[/url], now with many new features, improvements and much more.

[h3] Regarding localized voicelines [/h3]
To reduce the size of the addon, it will only contain the necessary elements, including materials, code, localization and English voicelines.
The addon, just like the original, does support localized voicelines. You can get them here: [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=1587455087]Workshop Link[/url]
